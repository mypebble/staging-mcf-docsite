What are your potential income streams and their associated costs?

All have a legal requirement of keeping financial records.

  • Fundraising - donations and campaigns
    • legal requirements e.g. your organisation must have charity status and donors must be UK taxpayers and eligible for Gift Aid - see HMRC guidelines
    • Case study - example from Arro customer that is working
    • Arro’s Donations and Campaigns modules help you with this
  • Commercial trading - lettings and services
    • legal requirements e.g. what sort of business or event should you allow or not allow
    • Case study - example from Arro customer that is working
    • Arro’s Lettings and Services modules help you with this.
    • Arro’s Social Media Campaigns help you with this.
  • Grants - e.g. grants
    • legal requirements - restrictions on how you spend the money
    • Case study - example from Arro customer that is working
    • Arro’s Grants module helps you with this
  • Sponsorship and collaboration with local business and other organisations
    • legal requirements - are there any restrictions on what you can spend the sponsorship on, what is the pay-back?
    • Case study - example from Arro customer that is working
    • Arro’s Sponsorship module helps with this
    • Arro’s Social Media Campaigns help with this
  • School to School collaboration - sharing facilities and services
    • legal requirements - approved reasons for sharing/hiring, division of profit e.g. school with building gets 80%, other school gets 20%, or equal 50%. How is it accounted.
    • Case study - example from Arro customer that is working
    • Arro’s Services and Lettings module helps with this.
