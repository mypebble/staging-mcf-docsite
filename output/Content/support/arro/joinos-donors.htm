Help on Supporting Projects

Tip: You can view screens on this page at full size by clicking on the thumbnail image.

Liking, donating, volunteering and sponsoring through the give.joinos.com page
School projects aim to increase what the school offers its pupils and you can help the school meet its projects aims in a number of ways. You can show support by liking the school projects and entering supportive comments, you can donate money to the projects, give your time by becoming a volunteer for the projects or by sponsoring school projects.

The school project’s give.joinos.com is a web page where you can easily give to a school or academy project.

Access the page

You can access this web page directly from the school or academy website, or if you know the URLAn address for an item on the Internet. you can type this into the browser directly.

Joinos - give page elements

Header

The header contains the project title and image, a link to click to Find out more about the project. More information about the project is displayed in the Our Project Story section of the project Joinos page. The header also displays the amount raised for the project so far along with a Make a Donation button, making it easy for people to donate to the project and increase the amount raised. The header also includes links to forms for people to fill in making it easy for them to become a volunteer or a sponsor for the project.

Project Story

The project story tells people what the project is for. This includes its aims and objectives, and what benefits this will bring both to pupils and the local community. Alongside the story is a button for people to like the project and leave supportive comments, ways for people to get involved, by donating, volunteering or enquiring about sponsorship, and ways for people to spread the word by sharing information about the project on social networks such as Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn.

Donations

A list of donations that have been made for the project is displayed in this section.

Donations can be any amount, can be a single one-off donation or a monthly recurring donation and can include Gift Aid if you are a UK taxpayer. If you are a UK taxpayer, the UK government can provide relief on the tax paid on your donation, thereby increasing the donation amount by 25%. If you are an ordinary rate taxpayer, this costs you nothing. If you are a higher rate taxpayer, you can receive tax refunds of the difference between the rate you pay and the basic rate of tax on the donation. Gift Aid is a win-win situation. So if you are a taxpayer in the UK, please consider signing up for Gift Aid when you make your donation.

Footer

Clicking Sponsorship will display the project’s sponsors, explaining the benefits to you of sponsoring, and illustrating the difference that sponsors make to the project. You can also click a link here if you are interested in sponsoring the project. And you can enter your email address to subscribe to the mailing list if you want to receive updates on the project and its progress.

How do I ...

Like a project?
  1. Click Like our Project.
  2. The Thanks for liking our project! box appears.
  3. If you want to leave a comment:
    1. Enter your Full name.
    2. If you don’t want your name to be visible alongside the comment, select Hide my name from the public.
    3. Enter your Email address.
    4. Enter your Comment.
    5. Click Send.

      6. Your comment will be displayed on the project Joinos page after it has been approved.,

  4. If you don’t want to leave a comment:
    • Click the close button on the Thanks for liking our project! box.
Make a donation?
  1. Click Make a Donation at the top of the page, or click Donate under Get Involved in Our project story.

    2. The How much would you like to donate? screen opens.

  2. Enter the Amount you want to donate.

    3. The amount defaults to £10 but you can change this to any amount you want to donate.

  3. By default, the transaction fee check box is selected so that the school will receive the full donation which you have entered in the Amount box. If you don’t want to pay the transaction fee, clear this check box.
  4. By default, Single Donation is selected for a one-off donation. If you want to make a donation which recurs monthly, select Monthly Donation.
    • If you have selected Monthly Donation, choose which date in the month you want to make the donation.
  5. Enter your Title, First Name and Last Name.
  6. Enter your Email Address. This is needed so that your donation receipt can be emailed to you.
  7. Enter your postal Address
  8. If you are a UK taxpayer, you can boost your donation by 25% by allowing the school or academy to reclaim 25% of the donation amount from HMRC. To do this, select the checkbox, I want to Gift Aid this donation in the Gift Aid my donation box.

    9. Note: By selecting the check box and entering your email address you are making a legal declaration of your tax paying status.

  9. If you want to donate anonymously, select the check box I’d like my identify to be hidden from others.
  10. Click Continue.

    11. Follow the instructions on the payment screen to enter your payment.

Enquire about sponsorship?

There are three places on the page where you can enquire about sponsoring the school’s project.

  • The header, by clicking Sponsorship.
  • Our project story, by clicking Sponsor.
  • The footer, by clicking sponsor our school.

In all cases, you need to fill in the Want to become a sponsor? form, by entering your Name, Telephone number and Email address. Then click Submit.

Volunteer to help the school?

There are two places on the page where you can enquire about volunteering to assist with the school’s project.

  • The header, by clicking Volunteering.
  • Our project story, by clicking Volunteer.

In both cases, you need to fill in the Volunteer form, by entering your Name and Email address. Then click Submit.

Keep up-to-date with what’s going on?
  • Enter your email address in the Keep up to date box on the Joinos page.
Share the project on social media?
  • Click one of the icons beneath Spread the Word to log on and share information about the school project.
