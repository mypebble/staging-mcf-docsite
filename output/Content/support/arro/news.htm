News

You can create and manage the news and media articles about the school, using the News option on the Navigation bar.

To add a news article

  1. Open News > + New news article.
  2. Enter the article Title.
  3. Select an image for the article, by clicking Select Image.

    4. Note: You can select an image from any of the preset images in Arro, or an image you have uploaded for any project. For more information on uploading images for your projects, see Projects.

  4. Select an image from the images that are displayed.
  5. Enter the Body text for the news article.

    6. Use the rich text editing features - B (embolden text), I (italicise text), H1 (heading 1), H2 (heading 2), # (ordered list) and (unordered list) - to lay out the text in your article as you want it to appear.

    Tip: Use C to clear formatting.

  6. To save the details:
    • To save the details and publish later, click Save draft.
    • To publish, click Publish.

      • Note: The Date is set as the present date when you click Publish. The Author is set as the logged-in Arro user.

To amend an existing news article

  1. Open News.
  2. Select the article Title and click it to display the article details.
  3. Amend the article Title, Image or Body text as required.

    4. Use the rich text editing features - B (embolden text), I (italicise text), H1 (heading 1), H2 (heading 2), # (ordered list) and (unordered list) - to lay out the text in your article as you want it to appear.

    Tip: Use C to clear formatting.

  4. To save the details:

    • To save the amended details and publish later, click Save draft.

      • Note: The published article is not updated until you click Publish.

    • To publish, click Publish.

      • Note: Articles are added and displayed on the Joinos Community Site News page in order of initial publication, so if you amend an article you have published already it won't appear as the first article published.

To archive articles

  1. Open News | Active.
  2. Select the check box to the left of the Title of the article to archive.

    3. Tip: You can select more than one article to archive at the same time or you can select the check box to the left of the word Title to select all the articles.

  3. Click Archive.

    4. Archived articles are listed on the Archived tab.

To restore archived articles

  1. Open News | Archived.
  2. Select the check box to the left of the Title of the article to archive.

    3. Tip: You can select more than one article to archive at the same time or you can select the check box to the left of the word Title to select all the articles.

  3. Click Restore.

    4. Restored articles are listed on the Active tab.
