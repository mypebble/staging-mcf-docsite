Getting help

There are two ways of getting help:

  • Using the Support website.
  • Talking to us.

Using the Support website

You’re already reading the help on the support website and this is where you can get more detailed information about using Arro.

  • To open the support website from Arro, click Help & Support on Arro‘s Navigation Bar.
    • Note:

    Screens may be shown in thumbnail size on the help centre website. Clicking an image works to toggle between full size and thumbnail. Try this out now:

    • Click on the thumbnail image to expand it to full size.
    • Click on the full size image to contract it to thumbnail size.

    Tip: You can only expand and contract one image at a time.

  • To search help, enter what you want to find in the Search box.

    • A list of help topics is displayed. For example, searching for Grant will give you a list of all support topics which contain the word Grant. The results are shown ranked so the first topic in the list has most references to Grant.

    • To select a topic to read, click the hyperlinked text.
    • To search again, click Back to Support and then enter a new search term or terms in the Search box.

    Note: The Search box will search every document on the Support website and may include documents that relate to other Pebble products if these include the search term or terms you have entered.

  • To open and display a help topic, click on the hyperlinked text. For example, to open Services and Lettings, click under Modules > Activities > Services & Lettings.

    • To return to the index of support topics for Arro, click Back to Support.
    • To give us feedback on the support, click How likely are you to recommend our INCOME GENERATION SUPPORT to other schools?
    • To find other related information, scroll to the foot of the topic and click any of the hyperlinked topics under More Information.
    • To print the help topic, scroll to the foot of the topic and click Print.

Talking to us

If you can’t find out what you need in the help centre website, you can ask us the query, using Arro‘s Conversations feature.

  • Click (bottom right of the Arro screen) to open the Conversations window.
  • Enter your message in the message box and click to send the message to Pebble.
