Services & Lettings

Earning income through your premises and selling school services in the wider community

You will find that you can hire out and use your school premises or property when it’s not being used during the school day and you can sell your in-house expertise in the community during out of school hours. For example, you can hire out your sports pitch or your drama studio when you aren’t using these facilitiesor, if you have IT knowledge or sport coaches within your knowledge bank at school, you can generate revenue by providing specialised assistance to organisations outside the school. This has two effects - it brings in more revenue to the school and it increases the community awareness about the school and its values and what it can offer the community.

In addition you can also trade your lettingsand services with other schools, filling in the gaps for each other and generating revenue for all of you.

 

The Services & Lettings module is where you manage the portfolio of your school’s premises for hireand potentially profitable services.

In the Services & Lettings module, you can:

  • Record and audit what you offer.
  • Market your portfolio.

You can build your portfolio by adding items from scratch or by adding them from categories (e.g. meeting room, playing field, gym, sports coaching, photocopying) and tailoring these to match your own offering. You can add images and terms and conditions documents, so all of the information about what is on offer is held in the same place.

How do I build a portfolio?

When you first begin to use this module the lettings and services dashboards will be empty and you are prompted to get started to build your portfolio. You can add items from scratch or select from categories and amend the details to match what your school offers.

Note: The process is the same, irrespective of whether you are building a portfolio of lettings or of services, the only difference is that you select the Lettings tab to get started building a portfolio of lettings and the Services tab to get started building a portfolio of services.

After first time use, the lettings dashboard displays the lettings you have chosen previously and their status and the services dashboard displays the services you have chosen previously and their status.

Status Available Actions
Incomplete Preview, Edit
Ready to publish Preview, Edit
Published Visit, Edit, Share

You can edit an item, by clicking Edit. You can see how it might look before it has been published by clicking Preview. You can visit the published page by clicking Visit and you can restore an archived item by clicking Restore.

You can create lettings and services either from a category or from scratch, by clicking + New Letting or + New Service.

Once you have published an item, you can Visit the published page, and Share the details on social media. You can select lettings or services which you want to feature most prominently on your published Services & Lettings page. If you don’t select any, we will select these automatically for you to ensure your page looks as good as it can.

Note:

Until you publish a letting or service then the link to your published page will display a message that the letting or service is private.

Create a new item from scratch

  1. Open Activities > Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Lettings or Services.

  3. Click + New Letting or + New Service.

  4. Click Custom Letting or Custom Service to display the Edit Your Letting or Edit Your Service screen.
  5. Enter the Letting or Service Name.

    6. Tip: Choose a title which is short and to the point but easily identifies the facility or service e.g. Gym, Meeting Room, Printing, Cleaning & Caretaking.

  6. Select the Letting Category or Service Category.
  7. Enter your Headline. This is a short description e.g. Fitness Gym with Weights, We have multiple meeting rooms, ideal for between 2 and 50 attendees, Photocopying and Scanning. We offer black and white, colour, single and double-sided copying.
  8. Select a Contact from the drop-down list of contacts.
  9. Enter the Contact Phone Number.
  10. Enter the Description. This is an expansion on the summary.
  11. To enter key features, click +Add a feature and enter the key feature in the box that appears. You can add up to five key features. These are the key selling points e.g. Bench Press, Disabled Access, Air Conditioning, Wifi, Tea and Coffee, perfect binding, leaflet assembly, laminating.
  12. Enter the Details for the hire. This is an expansion on the Headline description and can include full details of what is offered with the hire and whether there are conditions for the hire or activities which are suitable.
  13. When you are creating a letting, enter the Availability, for example, the dates and times the facility is available. If you are entering a service, you can arrange dates and times for the service ad hoc through the enquiries.
  14. Enter the Prices, for example, the rates of hiring, whether a deposit is required, what cancellation costs there are.
  15. If you have testimonials to include, enter the text in the Testimonial box. You should also enter the name of who has given the testimonial in the Endorser box.
  16. If you have someone who has used the facility and is willing to give a recommendation of it, you can enter their name in the Endorser box.
  17. Add images.

    3. Select the image to upload and click Open.
    4. Click Upload.

      6. Note: If you upload only one image this is the primary image by default.

How do I publish and promote my services and lettings portfolio?

You can choose the items you want to feature prominently on your published Services & Lettings Site, although you do not need to wait to finish creating your portfolio before you publish the items which you are ready to market. You can work on draft items and only publish the ones with complete information.

Once you have published an item, you can begin to promote it, by linking to your published page from the school website or by sharing its URL through social media.

Note: The published pages use a shade of dark blue. If you want to change this to a shade or colour that matches your school, please contact the Pebble Service team.

Select Featured Services and Lettings

  1. Open Activities > Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Lettings or Services.

  3. Click Select featured lettings or Select featured services.
  4. Click Select Lettings or Select Services.
  5. Click the items you want to select. The favourite icon turns fully grey when selected.
  6. Click Done.

Manage enquiries

When you publish and share details of your services and lettings people can enquire about booking them, either through phoning the contact phone number you have given or filling in the online booking form.

You can manage enquiries that come in via the online booking form on the Enquiries tab in the Services & Lettings module.

An enquiry has one of the following statuses:

Pending

The enquiry has come in via the online booking form on Your Services & Lettings Site. The terms of the booking need confirmation - this might or might not require enquiry will retain this status until correspondence between you and the enquirer is at the point where the booking is contractual.

Confirmed

You have confirmed the booking - this means that you have agreed with the enquirer the details of the hire - dates, times, payments and costs and the agreement is contractual.
Completed

The booking date has passed. You can now mark it as completed.

Note: If you have a booking that is repeating, leave it as Confirmed until the final date of the booking has passed.

Replying to enquiries

  1. Open Activities > Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the enquiry card to display the details of the enquiry message.
  4. Enter the reply into the Send a message box and click Reply.

    5. An email is sent to the enquirer with your reply. Any reply from the enquirer will be received in the Enquiries tab so you can follow all the correspondence there.

Confirming bookings

  1. Open Activities > Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the Pending enquiry card to display the details of the enquiry.
  4. Click Confirm.
  5. In the Confirm Enquiry screen, enter the Scheduled date for the booking and any Additional information you need to give the enquirer, such as types of payment accepted, when payment should be received, where to check in, etc.

    6. Note: If this is a repeating booking, enter the Scheduled date for the first booking.

  6. Click Confirm.

    7. The confirmation is sent to the enquirer and you now have a confirmed booking.

Completing bookings

  1. Open Activities > Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the Confirmed enquiry card to display the details of the booking.
  4. Click Complete.

    5. The booking is marked as completed.
