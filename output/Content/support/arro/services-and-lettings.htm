Services & Lettings

Tip: You can view screens on this page at full size by clicking on the thumbnail image.

Earning income through your premises and selling school services in the wider community
You will find that you can hire out and use your school premises or property when it’s not being used during the school day and you can sell your in-house expertise in the community during out of school hours. For example, you can hire out your sports pitch or your drama studio when you aren’t using these facilitiesor, if you have IT knowledge or sport coaches within your knowledge bank at school, you can generate revenue by providing specialised assistance to organisations outside the school. This has two effects - it brings in more revenue to the school and it increases the community awareness about the school and its values and what it can offer the community.
In addition you can also trade your lettingsand services with other schools, filling in the gaps for each other and generating revenue for all of you.

The Services & Lettings module is where you manage the portfolio of your school’s premises for hireand potentially profitable services.

In theServices & Lettings module, you can:

  • Record and audit what you offer.
  • Market your portfolio.

You can build your portfolio by adding items from scratch or by adding them from categories (e.g. meeting room, playing field, gym, sports coaching, photocopying) and tailoring these to match your own offering. You can add images and terms and conditions documents, so all of the information about what is on offer is held in the same place.

How do I build a portfolio?

When you first begin to use this module the lettingsand servicesdashboards will be empty and you are prompted to get started to build your portfolio. You can add items from scratch or select from categories and amend the details to match what your school offers.

Note: The process is the same, irrespective of whether you are building a portfolio of lettings or of services, the only difference is that you select the Lettings tab to get started building a portfolio of lettings and the Services tab to get started building a portfolio of services.

First time use

  1. Open Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Lettings or Services.

    3. The Get Started screen is displayed.

  3. To watch an introductory video about services and lettings, click the Play button .
  4. To begin creating your portfolio, click Get Started.
    5. When you are creating a portfolio of lettings

    The Select Your Lettings page is displayed. This shows categories, for generic facilities.

    1. Click Select on the categories which match what you have and would like to let.
    2. Click Continue.

      3. Details for the first category you selected are displayed for you to amend and complete. The others you selected are added to the lettings dashboard as Incomplete so you can edit these in turn after the first.

      Note: If you want to change the categories you chose, you can click Back and can then click Back to selection to return to the selection screen and choose different categories.

    When you are creating a portfolio of services

    The Select Your Services page is displayed. This shows categories for generic facilities.

    1. Click Select on a category which matches the service you would like to sell.

      2. The details for the category you selected are displayed for you to amend and complete.

      Note: If you want to change the category you chose, you can click Back and can then click Discard to return to the selection screen and choose a different category.

  5. Edit the details.

    6. Note: To make an item ready to publish, you must add a contact, a contact phone number and at least one image.

    1. Amend the details so these describe what you are offering.
    2. Select a user from the Contact drop-down list and enter a Contact Phone Number.
    3. Add images.
      Adding images for lettings
      1. Click + Add Image to add an image.
      2. Click Choose a file.
      3. Select the image to upload and click Open.
      4. Click Upload.

        5. Tip: If you upload an image you didn’t mean to, select the image. click Delete and confirm the deletion when prompted.

      5. If you upload more than one image, select which image is the primary image.

        6. Note: If you upload only one image this is the primary image by default.

      Adding images for services

      You can choose only one image to represent your service for hire.

      Either

      • Click one of the three Recommended Images to be the image that you use to advertise your service.
      • Click All Images and then choose an image you want to use to advertise the service.
      • Upload your own image by clicking +Add Custom Image.
        • Click Choose a file.
        • Select the image to upload and click Open.
        • Click Upload.

          • Tip: If you upload an image you didn’t mean to, select the image. click Delete and confirm the deletion when prompted.

  6. To save the details:
    • To save the details and publish later, click Save and publish later.
    • To publish now, click Publish now.

Create a new item from scratch

  1. Open Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Lettings or Services.

  3. Click +New Letting or +New Service.

  4. Click Select on Custom Letting or Custom Service to display the Edit Your Letting or Edit Your Service screen.
  5. Enter the Letting or Service Name.

    6. Tip: Choose a title which is short and to the point but easily identifies the facility or service e.g. Gym, Meeting Room, Printing, Cleaning & Caretaking.

  6. Enter your Summary. This is a short description e.g. Fitness Gym with Weights, We have multiple meeting rooms, ideal for between 2 and 50 attendees, Photocopying and Scanning. We offer black and white, colour, single and double-sided copying.
  7. Select a Contact from the drop-down list of contacts.
  8. Enter the Contact Phone Number.
  9. Enter the Description. This is an expansion on the summary.
  10. Enter the Key Features. These are the key selling points e.g. Bench Press, Disabled Access, Air Conditioning, Wifi, Tea and Coffee, perfect binding, leaflet assembly, laminating.
  11. Enter the Details for the hire. This is an expansion on the Description and can include full details of what is offered with the hire and whether there are conditions for the hire or activities which are suitable.
  12. When you are creating a letting, enter the Availability, for example, the dates and times the facility is available. If you are entering a service, you can arrange dates and times for the service ad hoc through the enquiries.
  13. Enter the Prices, for example, the rates of hiring, whether a deposit is required, what cancellation costs there are.
  14. If you have testimonials to include, enter the text in the Testimonial box. You should also enter the name of who has given the testimonial in the Endorser box.
  15. If you have someone who has used the facility and is willing to give a recommendation of it, you can enter their name in the Endorser box.
  16. Add images.

Manage enquiries

When you publish and share details of your services and lettings people can enquire about booking them, either through phoning the contact phone number you have given or filling in the online booking form.

You can manage enquiries that come in via the online booking form on the Enquiries tab in the Services & Lettings module.

An enquiry has one of the following statuses:

Pending

The enquiry has come in via the online booking form on Your Services & Lettings Site. The terms of the booking need confirmation - this might or might not require enquiry will retain this status until correspondence between you and the enquirer is at the point where the booking is contractual.

Confirmed

You have confirmed the booking - this means that you have agreed with the enquirer the details of the hire - dates, times, payments and costs and the agreement is contractual.
Completed

The booking date has passed. You can now mark it as completed.

Note: If you have a booking that is repeating, leave it as Confirmed until the final date of the booking has passed.

 

Replying to enquiries

  1. Open Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the enquiry card to display the details of the enquiry message.
  4. Enter the reply into the Send a message box and click Reply.

    5. An email is sent to the enquirer with your reply. Any reply from the enquirer will be received in the Enquiries tab so you can follow all the correspondence there.

Confirming bookings

  1. Open Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the Pending enquiry card to display the details of the enquiry.
  4. Click Confirm.
  5. In the Confirm Enquiry screen, enter the Scheduled date for the booking and any Additional information you need to give the enquirer, such as types of payment accepted, when payment should be received, where to check in, etc.

    6. Note: If this is a repeating booking, enter the Scheduled date for the first booking.

  6. Click Confirm.

    7. The confirmation is sent to the enquirer and you now have a confirmed booking.

Completing bookings

  1. Open Services & Lettings.

  2. Select Enquiries.
  3. Click the Confirmed enquiry card to display the details of the booking.
  4. Click Complete.

    5. The booking is marked as completed.

    3. When you publish the service or letting, the PDF Info Pack is available on the service or letting page to download.

Downloading files

  1. Open Services & Lettings.> Files.
  2. Click the File Name and it automatically downloads to your PC.

Removing files
