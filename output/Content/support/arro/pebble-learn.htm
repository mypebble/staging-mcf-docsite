Using Pebble Learn

Pebble Learn is a flexible and convenient way to access your Income Generation training. When you purchase a subscription to Arro, we walk you through all you need to know about setting up and using Arro to manage your projects and income generation. We also enroll you in our online training platform, Pebble Learn. Once your email has been verified by us, you will be able to access your online training courses from the Pebble Learn option on Arro's Navigation bar.

Accessing Pebble Learn

Note: Your Arro and Pebble Learn accounts are separate, so you will need to create a Pebble Learn account even although you already have an Arro account.

  1. Either open Arro > Learn or go to learn.mypebble.co.uk
  2. Sign up or Log on to Pebble Learn:
    3. If you are a new user, click Sign up or Enroll now

    Your Pebble Learn

    1. Enter your Full Name.
    2. Enter your Email Address.
    3. Enter your Password.
    4. Enter your password again in the Confirm Password box.
    5. Select the check box to agree to Teachable's and Pebble's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
    6. Click Sign Up.

      7. You will receive an activation email.

      • Click the link in the activation email you receive as this will verify your account and Pebble will then authorise and release the relevant training courses to you.
    If you are an existing user, click Login.
    1. Enter your Email address.
    2. Enter your Password.
    3. Click Log in.

      4. The Pebble Learn Dashboard is displayed and you will see the training courses available to you. For example:

      Note: You will only see the courses you can access once your email has been verified by us.

Forget your password?

  1. Either open Arro > Learn or go to learn.mypebble.co.uk
  2. Click Login.
  3. Click Forgot password?.
  4. Enter your email address in the Email Address box and click Send Me Instructions.
  5. An email will be sent to you with a link for you to click to reset your password.

Logging off

  • At the top right-hand side of the Pebble Learn dashboard, click your user image and select Log Out.

Note: Logging out is important for your data security.

Finding courses

All courses that you can access will be available under My Courses at the top right-hand side of the Pebble Learn dashboard. You may not have access to more courses than you can see on the dashboard but if you do, use the Find a course search box to enter the name of a course to find it.

For example, find courses on grants by entering the word grant.

Taking courses

  1. To start a course, click the course image on the dashboard.

    2. Note: You can see how far you have progressed through a course by the % complete figure.

  2. On the Class Curriculum, click Start where you want to begin.

    3. Note: We recommend you access the lectures in the order they are presented.

    • If you are just starting a course this will be the Introduction to the course.
    • If you are picking up from where you left off, click the Start button against the lecture you left off.

      • Tip: You can click Start Next Lecture at the top to automatically start the next incomplete lecture. This is a quick way of picking up where you last left off.

  3. Each lecture can be made up of different parts. You may have:
    • Videos
    • Articles and Text
    • Quizzes and Questions
    • Resources to Download
    • Comment Boxes (so you can have a say)

    To make sure you get the most out of taking the course, try to use as many of these on offer as possible.

  4. When you are finished a lecture, click Complete and Continue.

    5. This saves your progress and move on to the next lecture.
