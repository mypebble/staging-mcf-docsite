Email Campaigns

The Importance of Email Campaigns

Email Campaigns are one of the most important ways of communicating with your contacts, sharing information and asking for help.

 

Email Campaigns is where you create and view any marketing campaigns you have sent to your contacts by email. You can work on campaigns you want to send out and save these as drafts until you are ready to go with them.

Email Campaigns help you to maximise your engagement with your contacts. You can run different campaigns, for example, to help you recruit volunteers or share information and generate excitement about your project.

If you choose to send an email campaign, the number of emails sent depends on the campaign you select. For example, the Monthly Newsletter campaign sends one email while the Hire Our Facilities campaign sends six. You choose the contacts to send the campaigns to and when more than one email is sent in a campaign you choose the timeline too.

After you have run an email campaign, it is listed on the Scheduled tab unless there is just one email as in the Monthly Newsletter and today's date was used to send the email. In that case it is listed on the Completed tab.

You can preview which emails have been sent in the campaign by clicking the Expand icon alongside the campaign Name.

The Drafts tab displays the email campaigns you have previously saved as drafts. You can work on them by clicking the draft name. You can preview which emails are scheduled to send in the campaign by clicking the Expand icon alongside the campaign Name. As well as a preview of the emails which are in the draft campaign, if you have changed your mind and no longer want to send the campaign, you can remove the draft campaign here.

Create an Email campaign

    1. Open Campaigns > Email.
    2. Click + New Campaign.
    3. Click Select on the campaign you want to send from the available campaigns displayed.

      4. Tip: There are a number of campaigns to choose from. Campaigns have different functions. For example, a campaign to tell your story is different from a campaign which asks for help or one which sends out a newsletter to stakeholders.

    4. Select the project from the list of available projects and click Next.

      5. Tip: If you can’t see the project and you expect to, go to Projects and ensure you have marked the project as active and have published the project Joinos page.

    5. Within the Details section:
      1. All contacts are automatically selected to receive the campaign. Select Click to change to expand the contact list and create your own recipient list. You can choose to exclude individual contacts within each group that is selected if you prefer to do it this way.
      2. Select the Email frequency:
        3. Once a month Every month
        Light touch Once per week
        Standard campaign Every three days
        High-touch campaign Every day
      3. Select the date for the First email.
    6. Within the Messages to send, preview and edit the message text if required.
      1. Click Preview/Edit on a message. The existing message text is displayed on the left and the Edit email is displayed on the right.
      2. If you need to change anything, click in the appropriate box on the right and make the change. You can amend text and also the image on the email header.
      3. When you have finished, click Back to campaign.
    7. To save the campaign as a draft to send later, click Save Draft. The campaign is listed on Drafts tab.
    8. To send the campaign straightaway, click Send. The campaign is listed on Scheduled tab or the Completed tab, depending on the number of emails in the campaign and the date of the first email.

Completing a draft email campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Drafts.
  2. Click the draft name you want to complete.

    3. The Details and Messages to send screen is displayed.

  3. Amend the details and messages as required.
  4. To come back and complete this campaign later, click Save Draft.

    5. To send the campaign, click Send.

Removing a draft email campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Drafts.
  2. Click the arrow > to the left of the campaign Name.
  3. From the drop-down, click Remove campaign.
  4. Confirm that you want to remove the campaign draft by clicking Remove.
