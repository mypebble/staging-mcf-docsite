Lettings

Tip: You can view screens on this page at full size by clicking on the thumbnail image.

Earning income through your premises
You will find that you can hire out and use your school premises or property when it’s not being used during the school day and this will have two effects - it brings in more revenue to the school and it increases the community awareness about the school and it’s values and what it can offer the community.
In addition you can also trade your lettings with other schools, filling in the gaps for each other and generating revenue for all of you.

The Lettings module is where you manage the portfolio of your school’s premises for hire.

In the Lettings module, you can:

  • Record and audit the lettings you offer.
  • Create check lists to qualify people applying to hire or lease school facilities.
  • Calculate the cost of sale per item offered.
  • Market your portfolio.

You can build your lettings portfolio by adding items from scratch or by adding lettings from a number of templates (e.g. meeting room, playing field, gym) and tailoring these to match your own offering. You can add images of the facilities and terms and conditions documents, so all of the information about the items on offer is held in the same place.

How do I build a lettings portfolio?

When you first begin to use this module it will be empty and you are prompted to build your lettings portfolio. You can add items from scratch or add them by selecting from a library of letting templates and amending the details to match your school’s facility.

Create a letting

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click +New Letting.

    3. To create a new letting, you can:

    • Base the new letting on an existing template from the letting library.
    • Create the letting, using a blank letting template.

Base a new letting on an existing letting template

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click +New Letting.

  3. Click Select to choose a letting template to base the new letting on.
  4. Amend the template details as required.

    5. Important! You must select a Contact and enter a Contact Phone Number in order to save the template as a new letting.

  5. Click Done.

    6. The new letting is built and the letting details are displayed.

  6. To add an image, click +Add Image, Choose a file and browse to select the image file. Then click Upload.

    7. Note: You can add a number of images for a letting and choose one to be the primary display image. See Manage images for a letting.

  7. Click Done.

Create a letting, using a blank letting template

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click +New Letting > Custom Letting to display the Create Letting screen.
  3. Enter the Letting Name.

    4. Tip: Choose a title which is short and to the point but easily identifies the facility e.g. Gym, Meeting Room.

  4. Enter your Tagline. This is a short description of the letting e.g. Fitness Gym with Weights, We have multiple meeting rooms, ideal for between 2 and 50 attendees.
  5. Select a Contact from the drop-down list of contacts.
  6. Enter the Contact Phone Number.
  7. Enter the Description. This is an expansion on the tagline.
  8. Enter the Key Features. These are the key selling points e.g. Bench Press, Disabled Access, Air Conditioning, Wifi, Tea and Coffee, etc.
  9. Enter the Details for the hire. This is an expansion on the Description and can include full details of the facilities offered with the hire and whether there are conditions for the let or activities which are suitable.
  10. Enter the Availability, for example, the dates and times the facility is available to hire.
  11. Enter the Prices, for eample, the rates of hiring, whether a deposit is required, what cancellation costs there are.
  12. If you have someone who has used the facility and is willing to give a recommendation of it, you can enter their name in the Endorser box.
  13. Click Done. .

Manage images for a letting

Once you have created the letting text, you can upload images to represent it.

  1. To add an image:
    1. Click +Add Image.
    2. Click Choose a file and browse to select the image file.
    3. Click Upload.
  2. To mark an image as the primary image:
    1. Select the image you want to be the primary image.
    2. Click Set As Primary.
  3. To remove an image:
    1. Select the image you want to delete.
    2. Click Delete.

Updating letting details

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click View on the letting you want to update.
  3. Click Edit.
  4. Amend the details as required.
  5. Click Done.

    6. Draft Pending shows for the letting details. This means that you have made changes you have not yet published. For information on publishing the letting, see Publish a letting.

How do I publish and promote my lettings portfolio?

Lettings are not published until you choose to publish them. You can choose up to three lettings to feature in prominently on your hire.joinos.com page, although you do not need to wait to finish creating your portfolio of lettings before you publish the lettings which are ready to be publicised. You can work on draft lettings and only publish the ones with complete information.

Once you have published a letting to hire.joinos.com, you can begin to promote your items for hire, by linking to your hire.joinos.com page from the school website or by sharing its URL through social media.

Select Featured Lettings

Note: You can select up to three facilities to be displayed more prominently on your school’s hire.joinos.com page. If you select less than three, we automatically fill the space with other lettings of yours.

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click Select Featured Lettings.
  3. Click Select Lettings.
  4. Click the lettings that you want to select. The favourite icon turns fully yellow when selected.
  5. Click Done.

Publish a letting

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Click View.
  3. Click Publish.

    4. The letting details are published on your hire.joinos.com page. And Published is displayed at the top of the letting details.

Promote and share your school lettings

You need to promote and share your lettings in order to make them work for the school. We offer different ways fo you to do this. Your school lettings page can be advertised on your school website. You can also share links to each letting for hire where appropriate. For example, if you are promoting your sports hall for out of school hour use, you can share the link to this letting for hire directly.

Sharing your school’s lettings page

  1. Open Lettings and hover the mouse over the link in blue displayed under Your Hire Page. For example:

    2. Note: You will only see this link once you have published at least one letting to this page.

  2. To share your school’s lettings page, right-click and select Copy link address from the menu that appears.
  3. To share your school’s lettings page on Facebook, click Facebook, log in to Facebook and share a post that links to the lettings page.
  4. To share your school’s lettings page on Twitter, click Twitter. Text is automatically created for you to tweet along with the link to your school’s lettings page. Log in to Twitter to tweet the text and the link.

Sharing individual lettings

  1. Open Lettings.
  2. Clive View on a letting.

    3. As soon as you publish a letting, the letting URL is shown at the top of the letting details page. For example:

  3. To share your letting for hire, copy the letting URL link to include in an email campaign, for example.
    • Highlight the link that is shown.
    • Right-click and select Copy from the drop-down menu that appears.
  4. To share your letting for hire on Twitter, click Twitter at the top of the letting details.

    5. Text is automatically created for you to tweet along with the link to the letting details page. Log in to Twitter to tweet the text and link.

  5. To share your letting for hire on Facebook, click Facebook at the top of the letting details, log in to Facebook and share a post that links to the letting details page.
