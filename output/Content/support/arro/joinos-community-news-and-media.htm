News & Media Pages

News & Media Index Page

When you click on News & Media at the top of the Joinos Community Site Home Page, the News & Media Index Page is displayed. This is where your community can see all your news about your school projects and income generation news.

Within the News module, you choose which news items you want to feature most prominently on this page.

Under the section Browse all news, you can read about all the other news items on the site.

How do you choose featured news items?

  1. Open News.
  2. Select the articles you want to feature highest on your Joinos Community Site News & Media Index page.
  3. Click Choose Featured Articles.

    4. The details for these articles are shown when you click News & Media from the Joinos Community Site Home page.

News Item Page

You can view a news item page by clicking on the article on the News & Media Index Page.

From the news page, users can easily share your article on social media.

How do you create news and media articles?

You can create and manage the news and media articles about the school, using the News option on the Navigation bar.

To add a news article

  1. Open News > + New news article.
  2. Enter the article Title.
  3. Select an image for the article, by clicking Select Image.
  4. Select an image from the images that are displayed.

    5. Note: All preset images in Arro and all images you have uploaded for any project are available to choose from. For more information on uploading images for your projects, see Projects.

  5. Enter the Body text for the news article.
  6. Select the article category for the article - this helps readers of the site know the type of article they are looking at before they click through to the detail.
  7. To save the details:
    • To save the details and publish later, click Save draft.
    • To publish, click Publish.

      • The publication Date is set to today's date and Author is the logged on Arro user.

To amend an existing news article

  1. Open News.
  2. Select the article Title and click it to display the article details.
  3. Amend the article text, image or category as required.

  4. To save the details:

    • To save the amended details and publish later, click Save draft.

      • Note: The published article is not updated until you click Publish.

    • To publish, click Publish.

      • Note: Articles are added and displayed on the Joinos Community Site News page in order of initial publication, so if you amend an article you have published already it won't appear as the first article published.

To archive articles

  1. Open News | Active.
  2. Select the check box to the left of the Title of the article to archive.

    3. Tip: You can select more than one article to archive at the same time or you can select the check box to the left of the word Title to select all the articles.

  3. Click Archive.

    4. Archived articles are listed on the Archived tab.

To restore archived articles

  1. Open News | Archived.
  2. Select the check box to the left of the Title of the article to archive.

    3. Tip: You can select more than one article to archive at the same time or you can select the check box to the left of the word Title to select all the articles.

  3. Click Restore.

    4. Restored articles are listed on the Active tab.
