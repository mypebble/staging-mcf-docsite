Social Media Campaigns

The Importance of social media campaigns

Social media campaigns are one of the most important ways of communicating with your contacts, sharing information and asking for help.

Social media campaigns is where you create and view any marketing campaigns you have shared on Twitter.

If you choose to run a social campaign using Twitter, you can view the tweets that have been tweeted or are scheduled to tweet on the Twitter tab. Twitter campaigns contain tweets that will automatically be tweeted at 7 am, noon and 8 pm on consecutive days.

Create a campaign

    1. Open Campaigns > Social media > + New campaign.

    2. If this is the first time you have used Twitter from within Arro, you must log in and authorise Pebble to tweet for you.

    3. Authorising Pebble to tweet for you?

      You are asked to authorise Pebble to post tweets on your behalf. You only need to do this once.

      • If you are already logged in to Twitter:
        • Click Authorize App.
      • If you are not already logged in to Twitter:
        • Enter your school Twitter Username or email.
        • Enter your school Twitter Password.
        • Click Sign In.

      Authorisation is recorded within your school Twitter account and you are directed back to Arro which is redisplayed.

    4. You are presented with some pre-populated tweets for the campaign. Tweets are made three times a day at 7 am, noon and 8 pm GMT on successive days.

      1. To change tweet text, click in a Tweet box and amend the text.
      2. To add a tweet, click New Tweet to add a blank box at the foot of the list of tweets and then enter the text you want to tweet.
      3. To remove a tweet from the list, click X alongside the tweet.
    5. When you have set up the tweets for the campaign, click Schedule.
    6. Enter the First Tweet Date.

      7. This defaults to today’s date but you can set it to be what you want so you can create a number of Twitter campaigns and schedule them so that they don’t overlap.

    7. Click Start Tweeting.

      8. Click Back to Dashboard.

    Note:

    The schedule of tweeting depends on the Start Date and the time of day you click Start Tweeting. For example, if you schedule three tweets tomorrow these will be tweeted at 7 am, noon and 8 pm on the same day. If you select today’s date as the Start Date but click Start Tweeting at 11 am, the tweets will go out at noon, 8 pm and 7 am the next day.

    If you have scheduled a Twitter campaign and need to amend or cancel the tweets, contact Pebble for assistance.
