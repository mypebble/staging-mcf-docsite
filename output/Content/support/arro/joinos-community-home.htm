The Importance of Community Engagement

In order to achieve your school vision you need to engage your community and garner their support in what you are doing. To make it easy for you and for your community, we are providing the Joinos Community Site as the go-to hub for information about your school and its Income Generation goals. This is where your community will find all the most up-to-date news about your projects, your fund-raising goals and progress, your sponsors and volunteers, your services and lettings, your shop and your ...

You can easily link to your community site from your school website and vice versa, ensuring that the community can access the appropriate information for their needs. The school website tends to be for parents and pupils while the school’s Joinos Community Site is for everyone who wants to help your school succeed in its aspirations.

The Community Site is accessible by clicking on the Joinos Community Site option on the Navigation Bar within Arro. We’ll take a quick look through the different elements it has so you can familiarise yourself with this.

 

Like any Home Page, the Joinos Community Site Home Page is where you can access everything else on the site. Across the top is a menu of options that let you access and view all the other pages on the Joinos Community Site.

Making it easy for your community to engage

Links make it easy for anyone to indicate interest in donating, volunteering or sponsorship by clicking on the relevant links on the page.

From the menu across the top of the Home Page, you can access all the other pages on the site - Projects, Supporters, News & Media, About our School, Contact.

Members of the community can easily donate by clicking the Donate button.

They can keep up-to-date with what’s going on in your school and its projects by entering their email address in the Keep up to date box.

There’s also a link for people to generate donations when they shop online through goraise.co.uk.

When you begin to use Arro you are automatically set up with a GoRaise account, and can use a marketing campaign to encourage your stakeholders to sign up and use GoRaise to make online purchase. If they do this, then when they shop with an online retailer who has signed up to GoRaise, a donation is made by the retailer via GoRaise to the school.

 

Because it is so important that it is easy for people to do these things, we have included all of these links on every page on the Joinos

How do you update the items on the Home page? You can do this by clicking on the News option on the Navigation bar. For more information, see News & Media Pages.
