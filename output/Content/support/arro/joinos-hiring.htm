Hiring through the school’s hire.joinos.com page
Your school has facilities (buildings and specialist equipment) and services (printing and IT services) which you can make use of and earn your school more funds.

The school’s hire.joinos.com page is a web page where you can see what is available for hire at the school or academy.

Access the page

You can access this web page directly from the school or academy website, or if you know the URLAn address for an item on the Internet. you can type this into the browser directly.

Joinos - hire page elements

The Web Page has a number of elements. The following is an example of a hire facilities page to help you recognise the different parts.

Header

The header identifies the school.

Featured Services & Facilities for Hire

This section is where you can see which facilities and services are for hire and view details for individual ones.

Support the School

Find out more about the school and its aims by reading this section which includes current projects that are ongoing at the school.

Footer

The page itself is powered by Pebble software.

Joinos - Hire a facility or a service

  1. In the Featured Services and Facilities for Hire section, locate what you are interested in hiring and click View Details.

    2. The detailed screen for the facility is displayed.

    For example, if you click View Details on the school’s Sports Hall, the Sports Hall details page is displayed.

    This is where you can find out all the details about what’s on offer, hours of availability, description of what’s available and see pictures of what you are hiring. You can also see information about what hiring will mean to the school and why you should hire from the school.

    • You can download a PDF Information Pack about the facility or service if this is available by clicking Download Info Pack.
  2. Click Request to Book Online to make a booking.
