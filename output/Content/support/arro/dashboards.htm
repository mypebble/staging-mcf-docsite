Dashboard

The Arro dashboard appears when you first log on to Arro, and is also accessible from the Navigation bar, by clicking Dashboard.

This is where you can quickly and easily see how your projects are progressing.

Your community

You can see how many stakeholders, sponsors and volunteers you have gained during this month, and compare this to the past few months to see at a glance how well you are doing.

Actions

This section outlines what actions you need to take next to continue maximising your income generation potential for you school projects.

You can easily complete the required actions by clicking on the button alongside the action. For example, if the action is to find a grant to apply for, click on the Find a Grant button.

Spread the word

This section gives you an overview of your campaigns to promote your community page. When you run a campaign, five emails are sent over five weeks to the stakeholders you select when you prepared the campaign.

This section lists the campaigns you have currently on the go. You can see the start date of each campaign and from the campaign bar you can see how many people are opening or not opening your campaign emails.

This gives you an indication of how well the campaign is doing. For further information, click on the questions above the bar. For example, if your bar shows red, click on Why is this red? for tips on how to increase your open rate. Changing the email subject can have a big impact on this rate.

At the top of this section you get the statistics for the average click rateIndicates the number of times the link to your community page has been clicked. This can be from a hyperlink in an email during a marketing campaign, for example.. and average open rateThe number of times a marketing campaign email has been opened. of your campaign emails. You can click on Get more clicks or Get more opens for tips on how to get more people engaging with your campaign.

In order to promote your campaign using Twitter, click Connect Twitter.

Prompts

At the foot of the right hand side is an area with prompts for things which are helpful to get more engagement. For example, if someone has donated some money to the project, you can thank them directly from here by clicking Say Thanks. Other prompts might be for sharing your success, viewing other school success stories or finding out how you can get more people to view your community page.
