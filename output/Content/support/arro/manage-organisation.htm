Manage your Organisation Details

You can apply your school’s logo to your Joinos Community Site so it is easily recognisable as an extension to your school's website. You can add and amend text that captures your school’s motto, purpose, vision, mission statement and aims.

How do you manage the organisation details?

You enter and update these details from the Profile menu option in Arro. You may only need to enter these details once, but should any of the details change (for example, if you become an academy and your school name changes) you can easily update the details by using this option.

To enter or amend organisation details

  1. Select Profile from the drop-down menu under your username.

  2. In the Organisation Profile screen, enter the details that you want.

  3. Enter your school Name.
  4. Select the Primary contact.
  5. Enter the school Phone number.
  6. Enter the school Address.
  7. Enter the details you want people to read about the school in the About your organisation box.

    8. Tip: We recommend you keep this to three paragraphs at most.

    Note: You must enter details in the About your organisation box in order for the About our school page to appear on the Joinos Community Site.

  8. Enter the Elevator pitch. This is a short piece of text that summarises your school. It might be your school’s motto e.g. Be Prepared.
  9. Enter the school’s Facebook URL.
  10. Enter the school’s Twitter URL.
  11. Enter the school’s Linkedin URL.
  12. To select fundraising partners for your organisation:
    • Select the check box for each affiliate you want to select and enter the URL for the affiliate in the box alongside. For example,

  13. To add sponsors for your organisation, click + Add Sponsor.

    14. The Upload Sponsor info form appears.

    1. Enter the sponsor's Name.
    2. Enter a URL to Link to Sponsor if they have a website, blog or social media account.
    3. Enter a description of the sponsor in the About section.
    4. If you want the sponsor to be the featured sponsor on your Joinos Community Site, select Featured. This will give them the most prominent position in the display of sponsors.
    5. To upload an image, click Browse to select the image and click Open.
    6. Click Create.
  14. Click Save.

    15. The organisation details are automatically updated on your Joinos Community Site.

To upload your school logo

  1. Select Profile from the drop-down menu under your username.

  2. Alongside Upload logo, click Browse to locate your school’s logo image file.
  3. Locate the image file and click Open.
  4. Click Upload.

    5. The logo is automatically updated on your Joinos Community Site.
